Useful Resources

Tristan Goodell | 12 Oct 2018

A list of useful platforms, services, and more for anyone interested in web development or something similar.

Online Services

Digital Ocean - Affordable and Flexible Hosting. Supports One-Click Apps, Distribution, and Custom isos for their Droplet suite. Furthermore, they have a variety of other products to choose from. [$5/month+]

Namecheap - Cheap domains. Simple as that. [$1/year+]

Cloudflare - Superb DNS provider. Ensures that your site always has HTTPS and loads quickly to anyone in the world. [free+]

Github Student Developer Pack - Excellent way to start an online presence if you have a .edu email. Includes a free .me domain from Namecheap, $50 in credit for Digital Ocean, and more.

Zoho Business Email - A great way to get free custom domain emails for your website. You can get up to 25 accounts for free as noted in the blog post here. [free+]

Android Apps

F-Droid - An alternative Android appstore full of FOSS apps. A lot of high quality apps are showcased here.

K9 Material (unofficial) - The FOSS K-9 Mail modernized!
